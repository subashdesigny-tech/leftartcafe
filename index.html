Art Journey

How we deliver creativity and happy memories straight to your doorstep.

1. Choose Activity

Browse our catalog of themed workshops, classes, and DIY kits online. Pick one that matches your vibe.

2. Receive Kit

Our custom package arrives at your home. Packed inside are all required paints, brushes, canvas boards, and codes for guides.

3. Paint Together

Sip tea, put on soft music, open your paint pots, and create either individually or alongside friends and family.

4. Display Proudly

Varnish your artwork and display it on your wall, fridge, or bedside table to remind yourself of peaceful, happy hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our DIY paint kits, custom portraits, and workshop reservations.

Do I need previous art experience to join classes?

Absolutely not! Over 80% of our attendees are complete beginners. Our workshops and guides are structured to simplify lines, shadows, and blending so anyone can leave with a masterpiece they are proud of.

What is included inside a DIY Kit?

Every DIY kit includes pre-cut MDF boards or canvases, non-toxic pigment acrylic pots, customized styling brushes, reference card templates, sticker sheets, and access codes to step-by-step video instructions.

Can I book custom birthday parties or school sessions?

Yes, we plan events completely. We customize theme illustrations (such as space rockets or dragons), prepare personalized name cards, configure individual table easels, and supply custom medals and certificates for the kids.

Are the paint colors safe and washable?

Yes! All paints provided in kids kits and workshops are 100% non-toxic, water-based, and wash off skin and clothes easily with soap and warm water.
